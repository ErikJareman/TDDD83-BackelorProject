About admins:

If you are a teacher or tutor your school may have upgraded you to having an admin-account. This means that you have the possibility to create- and manage rooms.

When creating a room, you automatically become admin of that room and then you can promote other members with admin-accounts to make them admin of the room aswell. Furthermore you can also demote one of the currents admins in a room where you are admin.

To manage the admins, use the gear next to their names. If a member does not have a gear, it means that they do not have an admin-account.
